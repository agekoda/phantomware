Features

A deep dive into Phantomware's capabilities and specifications.

Core Features

  • **ESP32-S3 Microcontroller:** Powerful dual-core Xtensa LX7 processor with integrated Wi-Fi 4 (802.11n) and Bluetooth Low Energy 5 (BLE).
  • **Robust Power System:** Features a 3000mAh LiPo battery with USB-C charging via TP4056 and a precise MAX17055 fuel gauge for accurate battery monitoring. Includes overcharge and overcurrent protection for safety.
  • **Manual Power Switch:** Provides a true hardware shutdown for complete power control.

Inputs & Controls

  • **Navigation Buttons:** 6 tactile push buttons for intuitive navigation and control of the device interface.
  • **Infrared (IR) Transceiver:** Equipped with a TSOP75438 receiver and 3 high-power VSMY14940 LEDs for both receiving and transmitting IR signals, enabling universal remote control and IR data analysis.
  • **NFC Reader/Writer:** Integrates the PN532 NFC chip via SPI for reading, writing, and emulating NFC tags and cards.
  • **Temperature Sensor:** Features an MCP9701 for ambient or board temperature monitoring.

Visual & Audio Feedback

  • **High-Resolution Display:** A vibrant 240x320 TFT display (ST7789) for rich user interfaces and data visualization.
  • **Status LEDs:** Dual green and red LEDs provide immediate visual feedback on system state and activity.
  • **Piezo Buzzer:** Offers audible feedback for alarms, button presses, and even simple tunes.

Storage & Connectivity

  • **Micro SD Card Slot:** For expandable data logging, custom firmware storage, and file management.
  • **Shared SPI Bus:** The full SPI bus is shared efficiently between the PN532 NFC and SD card, with dedicated Chip Select (CS) pins for each.
  • **USB-C Port:** A modern USB-C port with CC line detection for reliable charging, data transfer, and serial communication.

Expandability & Debugging

  • **GPIO Expansion Pins:** Convenient breakout pins for connecting external modules and custom circuitry.
  • **Communication Breakouts:** Dedicated I²C, SPI, and UART breakout pins for easy integration with other sensors and peripherals.
  • **Maximized GPIO Utilization:** All relevant GPIOs are thoughtfully assigned to ensure maximum functionality and ease of use for developers.
