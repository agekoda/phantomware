About Phantomware

Phantomware is a versatile multi-function development board featuring the ESP32-S3 microcontroller. It integrates IR communication, NFC reading/writing, a rich 240x320 TFT display, an SD card reader, a full battery charging circuit with a MAX17055 fuel gauge and overcharge + overcurrent protection, 6 navigation buttons, and more — perfect for advanced IoT projects and prototypes.
